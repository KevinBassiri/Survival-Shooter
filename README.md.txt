Survival Shooter Project - Unity 3D

-Ran by selecting Unity3D and C#\Survival Shooter\Build\Survival Shooter.exe

-The goal is to survive as long as possible against stuffed animals

	+Contains sounds

	+Controls are WASD for movement, click to shoot enemies

-Points are acquired by defeating different enemies

-The following is controlled by C#

	+Player movement

	+Enemy AI

	+Camera

	+Enemy spawn points

	+Game over screen

	+Enemy animations

	+Player animations
